VISI & MISI

VISI

  • Creating an Islamic atmosphere in madrasas, achieving and having noble morals in accordance with the teachings of ahlus sunnah wal-jama'ah

MISI

  • 1. Organize activities on Islamic holiday
  • 2. Carry out worship practices in daily life according to the teachings of ahlus sunnah wa al-jama'ah.
  • 3. Give awards to students who excel
  • 4. Give additional lessons to students who need them
  • 5. Exemplify noble moral behavior in various ways
